Kiki Mordi


Nigerian journalist, media personality, filmmaker and writer.

Here's a time line of Kiki Mordi's life:

  • 1991 - Born in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria
    • 2013 - She started her career as a student broadcaster in Rhythm FM Benin City after dropping out of University. Shortly after she became Benin city’s radio darling bagging multiple awards as the midday show host in KU FM Benin
  • 2016 - She won the award of Outstanding Radio Program Presenter. (South-South) at the Nigerian Broadcasters Merit Awards.
    She was also nominated for the On-air Personality of the Year (Female) at the Scream All-Youth Awards.
  • 2017 - She became the Head of Presenting at Women Radio 91.7 n Lagos, Nigeria’s first radio station dedicated to women and their families. During her time at women radio, she played host to some of the finest women in leadership on the critical acclaimed “Woman Agenda” radio programme in partnership with the Mac Arthur Foundation that addresses the intersectionality of Gender and Corruption in Nigeria
    She also started an online petition to end police extortion and exploitation after some Nigerian policemen invaded their home and accused her and her boyfriend of being cultists.
  • 2019 - she was nominated for The Future Awards Africa in Prize for Journalism category.
    Kiki is the founder of the #DocumentWomen movement that aims at tackling the erasure of women and women’s stories and produced a documentary entitled “Life At The Bay” that documented the lives of women living in the remote island, Tarkwa Bay. The film has since been selected for Real Time International Film Festival, Lift Off Global Network and Africa International Film Festival. And counting.
    On 17 May 2019, the film directed by Nora Awolowo was selected by Real Time International Film Festival.
    6 October 2019, the film was selected to show at the 2019 Africa International Film Festival (AFRIFF).
  • 2020 - She won the People Journalism Prize For Africa.
    She won also the International Emmy Awards Current Affairs & News in the same year.

Kiki Mordi, born Nkiru Mordi is a multi award winning investigative journalist, a radio presenter and filmmaker.
She has worked in various capacities as presenter, writer, voice over artiste, filmmaker, reporter and an advocate for gender equality.
